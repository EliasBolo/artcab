A FEW WORDS

GEORGE KANELLAKOS

I have been a taxi driver for the last 29 years. I represent the modern generation of taxis as this was a family business that started many years ago.

I took over the baton with the main aim of upgrading the services offered with direct service in the Sparta area but also throughout Greece.

Professionalism, confidentiality and a positive attitude for a comfortable and safe trip are some of the elements that characterize me.

I use the English language in an excellent way, so that I can welcome the visitors who come to our country.

My creative mood gave birth to the idea of ​​organizing tours just for you and in the destination of your choice.

My goal is to ensure a pleasant atmosphere in a professional way, during the journey and of course to be by your side at every moment. Even on the most beautiful day of your life, your Wedding, I will be there to take you to the church with the new technology car and the appropriate clothing.

For me every destination is never a place, but a new way of seeing things!
